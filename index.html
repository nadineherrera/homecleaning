10-Minute Nightly Reset

Use this every evening to stop clutter and dishes from building up.

Kitchen

  • Load dishwasher or wash dishes
  • Wipe counters and stovetop
  • Put food away
  • Quick sweep of crumbs

House Reset

  • Pick up clutter
  • Fold blankets
  • Straighten pillows
  • Quick bathroom sink wipe

Pets + Laundry

  • Scoop litter boxes
  • Shake litter mats
  • Move or fold one small laundry load

20-Minute Bathroom Cleaning Method

Professional order: spray first, then clean from least dirty to most dirty.

Step 1
Spray shower, tub, sink, counters, and toilet. Add bowl cleaner to the toilet and let products sit.
Step 2
Clean mirrors and light fixtures while products work.
Step 3
Wipe sink, faucet, counters, and cabinet handles.
Step 4
Scrub the shower and tub.
Step 5
Clean the toilet bowl, seat, lid, base, and floor around it.
Step 6
Mop the floor on your way out of the room.

Daily Tasks

Small daily maintenance keeps the house from sliding back into overwhelm.

Kitchen + Home

  • Wash dishes or run dishwasher
  • Wipe counters
  • Wipe stovetop if used
  • Sweep crumbs
  • Complete 10-minute nightly reset

Pets + Laundry

  • Scoop litter boxes
  • Sweep litter area if needed
  • Run one small load of laundry if needed

Weekly Tasks

Use these as your standard house rhythm.

Kitchen

  • Mop kitchen floor
  • Clean microwave interior
  • Wipe appliance fronts

Bathrooms + Bedrooms

  • Complete 20-minute bathroom routine
  • Change bedding
  • Vacuum bedroom floors

Living Areas + Pets

  • Vacuum rugs and furniture
  • Dust surfaces
  • Vacuum litter areas
  • Clean around litter boxes

Biweekly Tasks

Every two weeks, clean the details that make the house feel finished.

  • Vacuum under furniture edges
  • Clean baseboards in high-traffic areas
  • Wash pet bedding
  • Clean window sills
  • Wipe interior doors and handles

Monthly Tasks

Monthly work prevents grime, odor, and small maintenance issues.

Kitchen

  • Clean refrigerator shelves
  • Clean dishwasher filter
  • Clean oven interior if needed
  • Clean refrigerator door seals

Bathrooms + House

  • Clean drains
  • Scrub grout if needed
  • Dust vents and air returns
  • Clean light switches and door handles
  • Wash trash cans

Maintenance

  • Replace or clean HVAC / AC filter
  • Check for plumbing leaks
  • Inspect basement for moisture, pests, or mold
  • Inspect washing machine hoses
  • Check sump pump pit if present

Quarterly Tasks

Every 3 to 4 months, do the bigger cleaning and home-system checks.

Deep Cleaning

  • Wash or mop walls in high-traffic areas
  • Clean baseboards throughout the house
  • Clean window tracks
  • Clean blinds
  • Wash curtains if applicable
  • Move furniture and vacuum underneath
  • Pull refrigerator out and clean behind it
  • Pull stove out and clean behind it

Maintenance Checks

  • Clean refrigerator coils
  • Inspect caulking around tubs, showers, and sinks
  • Run garbage disposal with ice and citrus
  • Check water heater for leaks or rust
  • Clean AC condensate drain line
  • Test GFCI outlets in kitchen, bathrooms, and garage
  • Inspect toilet flappers for silent leaks
  • Check window locks and tracks

Twice Per Year + Annual Homeowner Maintenance

These are the tasks that protect the house and reduce expensive surprises.

Twice Per Year

  • Clean gutters
  • Flush water heater
  • Clean dryer vent and dryer duct
  • Replace HVAC / furnace filters if not done monthly
  • Check attic or roof area for leaks
  • Inspect exterior siding and foundation
  • Check weather stripping on doors and windows
  • Clean exterior vents and exhaust fans
  • Lubricate garage door tracks and hinges
  • Declutter and reorganize garage and basement storage

Annual

  • Service HVAC system
  • Wash interior windows
  • Clean exterior windows
  • Deep clean basement
  • Clean garage walls
  • Clean inside cabinets and closets
  • Inspect smoke detectors and replace batteries
  • Inspect chimney if applicable
  • Reseal grout in showers if needed
  • Check sump pump operation
  • Clean range hood filters
  • Inspect deck, porch, driveway, and walkways
  • Check attic insulation and ventilation
